About our Puppies

I have an excellent history of health in my Shiba Inu. All my Shiba Inu are up to date in vaccinations and de-wormings. Vaccinations are done at 5, 7, 9 and 12 weeks of age. Top of the line vaccines are used and no corners are cut to give optimum coverage. Pups will come with an extensive health record to be taken to the new veterinarian for future care. I have received many compliments from buyers' veterinarians for my records and health care. My health care program saves buyers a lot of time and money!!

Every Shiba Inu puppy is raised lovingly with my children from birth which produces a much more sound and gentle temperament resulting in a much better family companion right from the start. This shows in the puppies we raise, as well as the resulting adult Shiba Inu. We do not believe in sacrificing anything in the process. Our days are spent interacting with our Shiba Inu, grooming, walking and playing (or even napping) I know the key to a rich relationship with any Shiba Inu is very dependent on good breeding and socialization. Every Shiba Inu is bred for excellent conformation, health, and temperament to become the best family companion available. In hopes that they will meet every expectation you have of them. When our puppies leave our care, they are HAPPY, HEALTHY, WELL-SOCIALIZED, and have had LOTS OF T.L.C.!It is our goal that they stay that way. Anything less just isn't good enough for our wonderful puppies!!

You are always welcome to pick up your new Shiba Inu Puppy here in Akeley, Minnesota, Come in, have a can of pop or a cup of coffee, and visit with us a while. We'd love to meet you, and show off our puppies! If you live far away, we can bring your new puppy to meet you as far as 60 miles from Akeley, MN, or we can offer safe shipping to you via the airlines, weather permitting.

We will require a deposit to hold your new Shiba Inu puppy, by 7 weeks of age, the remaining balance is due in full. At 8 weeks of age, your puppy will be ready for pickup. Shipping charges must be paid prior to shipment.

Deposits are non-refundable.
